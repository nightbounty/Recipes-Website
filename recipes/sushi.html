Sushi Recipe

Description

Sushi is a popular Japanese dish made from seasoned rice with fish, egg, or vegetables. A sushi roll is shaped inside a thin sheet of seaweed. Sushi comes from a Japanese word meaning "sour rice," and it's the rice that's at the heart of sushi, even though most Americans think of it as raw fish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Crab
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Sesame seeds
  • Nori seaweed sheets

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Cover one nori seaweed sheet with some rice
  3. Add crab to the nori sheet
  4. Add cucumber to the nori sheet
  5. Add avocado to the nori sheet
  6. Roll the nori sheet to form a sushi roll
  7. Cut the sushi roll in smaller size that you like
  8. Springle some seasame seeds on the sushi rolls
  9. Now eat and enjoy the fruit of your labour of your hardwork
